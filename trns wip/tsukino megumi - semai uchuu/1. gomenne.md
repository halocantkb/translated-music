## Sorry

*I can't become someone walking down the street*
暑中頬をたゆたうものを払って  
*Brushed away the tears that flowed in the heat*
花を絶え間なく枯らしてしまった  
*I left flowers withering without end*
玉虫色に光るものに見惚れていたの次はぼくの番  
*I was mesmerized by the iridescent glow, but it's my turn*

*Swimming in an empty tank isn't okay*
たとえばこれが悪夢なら目を覚まして  
*If this were a nightmare, I'd wake up*
月のようなきみを見ていれるだけだった  
*And I'll only be able to gaze upon you, just like the moon*
